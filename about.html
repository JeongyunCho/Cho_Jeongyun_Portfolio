Hola! My name is Diego Bernal and I'm passionate about Motion Graphics, Design and Illustration. I was born in Bogota, Colombia and I studied there Audiovisual Media and some courses of Graphic Design. However, my curiosity and passion for these fields led me to self-taught practices. In 2016, I came to Canada initially to learn English and to explore another culture. I decided then to enroll in the Interactive Media Design program at Fanshawe College as a way to immerse myself more in the new language as well as gaining other skills and work experience. I decided to challenge myself to pursue a career as Motion Graphics Designer here in Canada. "Tomorrow I'm going to make it better" - is my motto. I also like photography, films, and video games. In my free time I like to watch movies, series on Netflix or having a beer with friends. I also like going to barbecues, trying new food and exploring new places.
